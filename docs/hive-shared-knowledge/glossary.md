## Teaching & Learning

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **ACTFL Level** | American Council on the Teaching of Foreign Languages proficiency scale (Novice Low → Distinguished) | "Student is at Intermediate Mid" |
| **Competency** | A specific skill a student can demonstrate, not just knowledge | "Can order food in a restaurant" vs "knows restaurant vocabulary" |
| **Drill** | Structured practice with immediate feedback. Types: repeat, translate, match, fill_blank, sentence_order | "Let's do a translation drill" |
| **Session** | A single voice conversation with Daniela, typically 5-30 minutes | "In our last session..." |
| **Turn** | One exchange (student speaks → Daniela responds) | "After 10 turns, we covered greetings" |

---

## Syllabus & Curriculum

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Syllabus** | Teacher-customizable learning path for a class. Contains units and lessons. | "Spanish 101 syllabus" |
| **Curriculum Path** | A pre-built syllabus template. Gets cloned to create class syllabi. | "ACTFL-aligned Spanish curriculum" |
| **Unit** | A themed collection of lessons (e.g., "At the Restaurant") | "Unit 3: Travel & Transportation" |
| **Lesson** | A single learning objective within a unit | "Lesson: Ordering Coffee" |
| **Bundle** | A lesson paired with its drill practice. Teach concept → reinforce with drill. | "The 'Colors' lesson + its matching drill" |

## Intervention Controls

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Correction Timing** | When Daniela corrects errors: immediate, delayed, or on_request | "Use delayed correction for advanced students" |
| **Correction Depth** | How much explanation with corrections: minimal, moderate, comprehensive | "Set comprehensive depth for grammar explanations" |
| **Scaffolding Level** | How much help to provide: none, hints, guided, explicit | "Hints scaffolding for intermediate learners" |
| **Error Tolerance** | How strict about errors: strict, moderate, lenient | "Lenient tolerance focuses on communication" |
| **Interrupt Behavior** | When to interrupt student flow: never, critical_only, on_pattern | "Interrupt on pattern catches repeated errors" |
| **Pronunciation Handling** | How to address pronunciation: ignore, note, practice, drill | "Practice mode offers slow pronunciation" |
| **Micro-intervention** | A small, targeted teaching moment during conversation | "Quick correction without stopping flow" |
